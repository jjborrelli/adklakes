The Adirondack Park can be split into 5 large watersheds, effectively dividing up the sampling region.

Saint Francois River-Saint Lawrence River

A portion of the Saint Francois River-Saint Lawrence River watershed overlaps into the park at the northern edge.

This watershed has 127 lakes, representing 1.1338273 % of the total number of lakes in the park.

In Saint Francois River-Saint Lawrence River watershed only Upper Chateaugay Lake has had multiple samples of several water quality variables. Bradley Pond, Mountain Pond, North Twin Pond, South Twin Pond, and Peter Pond were each sampled once in the 80s. Upper Chateaugay Lake is also the only waterbody with a DEC boat launch, a hard surface launch.
